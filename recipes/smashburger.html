Smashburger

Description

Classic American style burger with a thin patty.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Pickles
  • Butter
  • Burgerbun
  • Neutral oil
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Form beef into small balls.
  2. Bring oil in pan to high temperature.
  3. Place beef balls onto pan and smash them flat.
  4. Cook to desired temperature.
  5. Add cheese on patties.
  6. Plate with bun and pickles.
