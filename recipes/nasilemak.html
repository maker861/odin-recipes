Nasi Lemak

Description

Nasi Lemak is a Malaysian dish which consists of fragrant coconut rice, a sweet and spicy anchovy sambal, roasted peanuts and other sides.

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice
  • Coconut Milk
  • Dried Anchovies
  • Eggs
  • Cucumbers
  • Thai Chilli
  • Lemongrass
  • Onions
  • Roasted peanuts
